Our Mission

Pokemom Restaurant one of hundreds of sites around the city that partner with our Retired and Senior Volunteer Program (RSVP)—serves a hearty, nutritious meal each day to about 1,000 hungry and homeless New Yorkers. The Pokemom also offers guests refuge from the streets, a safe place where they are welcomed, and connection to additional services.
